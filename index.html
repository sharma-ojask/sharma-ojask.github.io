About Me

I’m an undergraduate at Georgia Tech with experience spanning machine learning, data analytics, and product management. My work focuses on building technically rigorous systems while staying grounded in real-world impact.

I’ve worked across research labs, industry teams, and product roles, shaping how I think about modeling, evaluation, and deployment in human-centered systems.
